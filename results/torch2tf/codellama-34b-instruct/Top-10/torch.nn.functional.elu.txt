
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.functional.elu`:

1. `tf.keras.layers.ELU`
2. `tf.keras.activations.elu`
3. `tf.nn.elu`
4. `tf.keras.backend.elu`
5. `tf.keras.layers.LeakyReLU`
6. `tf.keras.activations.leaky_relu`
7. `tf.nn.leaky_relu`
8. `tf.keras.backend.leaky_relu`
9. `tf.keras.layers.PReLU`
10. `tf.keras.activations.prelu`
