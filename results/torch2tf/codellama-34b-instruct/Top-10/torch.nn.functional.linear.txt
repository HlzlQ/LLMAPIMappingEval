1. `tf.keras.layers.Dense`
2. `tf.keras.layers.Linear`
3. `tf.keras.layers.Flatten`
4. `tf.keras.layers.Reshape`
5. `tf.keras.layers.Permute`
6. `tf.keras.layers.RepeatVector`
7. `tf.keras.layers.Lambda`
8. `tf.keras.layers.Embedding`
9. `tf.keras.layers.Bidirectional`
10. `tf.keras.layers.TimeDistributed`
