
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.ReLU`:

1. `tf.keras.layers.ReLU`
2. `tf.keras.activations.relu`
3. `tf.nn.relu`
4. `tf.nn.leaky_relu`
5. `tf.nn.elu`
6. `tf.nn.selu`
7. `tf.nn.softplus`
8. `tf.nn.softsign`
9. `tf.nn.tanh`
10. `tf.nn.sigmoid`
