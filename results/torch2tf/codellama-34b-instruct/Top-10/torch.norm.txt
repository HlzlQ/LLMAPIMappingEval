
Here are ten possible mappings from PyTorch's `torch.norm` API to TensorFlow's APIs:

1. `tf.linalg.norm`
2. `tf.math.norm`
3. `tf.norm`
4. `tf.linalg.vector_norm`
5. `tf.math.vector_norm`
6. `tf.vector_norm`
7. `tf.linalg.matrix_norm`
8. `tf.math.matrix_norm`
9. `tf.matrix_norm`
10. `tf.linalg.tensor_norm`

Note that some of these APIs may not be exact replacements for PyTorch's `torch.norm`, but they should provide similar functionality.
