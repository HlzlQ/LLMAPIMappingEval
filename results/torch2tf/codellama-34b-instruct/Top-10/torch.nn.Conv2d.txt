
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for the same functionality as PyTorch's `torch.nn.Conv2d`:

1. `tf.keras.layers.Conv2D`
2. `tf.layers.conv2d`
3. `tf.nn.conv2d`
4. `tf.nn.depthwise_conv2d`
5. `tf.nn.separable_conv2d`
6. `tf.keras.applications.Conv2D`
7. `tf.keras.layers.DepthwiseConv2D`
8. `tf.keras.layers.SeparableConv2D`
9. `tf.nn.atrous_conv2d`
10. `tf.nn.conv2d_transpose`
