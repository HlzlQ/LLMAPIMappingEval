
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for the same purpose as PyTorch's `torch.nn.init.ones_`:

1. `tf.ones`
2. `tf.zeros`
3. `tf.fill`
4. `tf.constant`
5. `tf.Variable`
6. `tf.get_variable`
7. `tf.placeholder`
8. `tf.sparse_placeholder`
9. `tf.ragged_placeholder`
10. `tf.convert_to_tensor`
