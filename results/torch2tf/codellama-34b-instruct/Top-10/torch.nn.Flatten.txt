
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.Flatten`:

1. `tf.keras.layers.Flatten`
2. `tf.keras.layers.Reshape`
3. `tf.keras.layers.Permute`
4. `tf.keras.layers.RepeatVector`
5. `tf.keras.layers.Lambda`
6. `tf.keras.layers.Dense`
7. `tf.keras.layers.Conv2D`
8. `tf.keras.layers.MaxPooling2D`
9. `tf.keras.layers.AveragePooling2D`
10. `tf.keras.layers.GlobalAveragePooling2D`
