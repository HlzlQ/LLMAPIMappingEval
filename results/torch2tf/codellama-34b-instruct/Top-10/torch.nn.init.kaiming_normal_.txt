
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for Kaiming normal initialization:

1. `tf.keras.initializers.KaimingNormal()`
2. `tf.keras.initializers.HeNormal()`
3. `tf.keras.initializers.GlorotNormal()`
4. `tf.keras.initializers.VarianceScaling(scale=2.0, mode='fan_in', distribution='normal')`
5. `tf.keras.initializers.TruncatedNormal(mean=0.0, stddev=1.0)`
6. `tf.keras.initializers.RandomNormal(mean=0.0, stddev=1.0)`
7. `tf.keras.initializers.Orthogonal(gain=1.0)`
8. `tf.keras.initializers.Identity()`
9. `tf.keras.initializers.Constant(value=0.0)`
10. `tf.keras.initializers.Zeros()`
