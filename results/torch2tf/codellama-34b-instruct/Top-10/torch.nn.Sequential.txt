
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.Sequential`:

1. `tf.keras.models.Sequential`
2. `tf.keras.layers.Sequential`
3. `tf.keras.models.Model`
4. `tf.keras.layers.Layer`
5. `tf.keras.models.StackedRNNCells`
6. `tf.keras.layers.StackedRNNCells`
7. `tf.keras.models.Bidirectional`
8. `tf.keras.layers.Bidirectional`
9. `tf.keras.models.TimeDistributed`
10. `tf.keras.layers.TimeDistributed`
