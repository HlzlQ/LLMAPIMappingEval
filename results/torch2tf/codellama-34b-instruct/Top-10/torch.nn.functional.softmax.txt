
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.functional.softmax`:

1. `tf.keras.layers.Softmax`
2. `tf.keras.activations.softmax`
3. `tf.nn.softmax`
4. `tf.keras.backend.softmax`
5. `tf.keras.layers.Dense(units=1, activation='softmax')`
6. `tf.keras.layers.SoftmaxCrossEntropyWithLogits`
7. `tf.nn.softmax_cross_entropy_with_logits`
8. `tf.keras.backend.softmax_cross_entropy_with_logits`
9. `tf.keras.layers.SoftmaxCrossEntropyWithLogitsFromProbs`
10. `tf.nn.softmax_cross_entropy_with_logits_from_probs`
