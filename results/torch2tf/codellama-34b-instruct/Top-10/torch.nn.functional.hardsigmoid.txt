
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.functional.hardsigmoid`:

1. `tf.keras.layers.HardSigmoid`
2. `tf.keras.activations.hard_sigmoid`
3. `tf.nn.hard_sigmoid`
4. `tf.nn.relu6`
5. `tf.nn.leaky_relu`
6. `tf.nn.softmax`
7. `tf.keras.layers.Softmax`
8. `tf.keras.activations.softmax`
9. `tf.nn.sigmoid`
10. `tf.keras.activations.sigmoid`
