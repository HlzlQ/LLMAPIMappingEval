
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.PReLU`:

1. `tf.keras.layers.PReLU`
2. `tf.keras.layers.LeakyReLU`
3. `tf.keras.layers.ParametricReLU`
4. `tf.keras.layers.ThresholdedReLU`
5. `tf.keras.layers.Softplus`
6. `tf.keras.layers.Swish`
7. `tf.keras.layers.Mish`
8. `tf.keras.layers.Sigmoid`
9. `tf.keras.layers.Tanh`
10. `tf.keras.layers.ReLU6`
