
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.functional.tanh`:

1. `tf.math.tanh`
2. `tf.keras.layers.Activation('tanh')`
3. `tf.keras.activations.tanh`
4. `tf.nn.tanh`
5. `tf.nn.activation_ops.tanh`
6. `tf.nn.relu_tanh`
7. `tf.nn.leaky_relu_tanh`
8. `tf.nn.softmax_tanh`
9. `tf.nn.sigmoid_tanh`
10. `tf.nn.hard_tanh`
