
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.optim.RMSprop`:

1. `tf.train.RMSPropOptimizer`
2. `tf.keras.optimizers.RMSprop`
3. `tf.compat.v1.train.RMSPropOptimizer`
4. `tf.estimator.RMSProp`
5. `tf.keras.optimizers.Adam` with `amsgrad=True`
6. `tf.keras.optimizers.Nadam`
7. `tf.keras.optimizers.SGD` with `momentum=0.9` and `nesterov=True`
8. `tf.keras.optimizers.Adagrad`
9. `tf.keras.optimizers.Adadelta`
10. `tf.keras.optimizers.Adamax`
