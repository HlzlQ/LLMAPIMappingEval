2. `tf.keras.initializers.Uniform`
3. `tf.keras.layers.Dense.kernel_initializer`
4. `tf.keras.layers.Conv2D.kernel_initializer`
5. `tf.keras.layers.LSTMCell.kernel_initializer`
6. `tf.keras.layers.GRUCell.kernel_initializer`
7. `tf.keras.layers.SimpleRNNCell.kernel_initializer`
8. `tf.keras.layers.Embedding.embeddings_initializer`
9. `tf.keras.layers.BatchNormalization.gamma_initializer`
10. `tf.keras.layers.BatchNormalization.beta_initializer`
