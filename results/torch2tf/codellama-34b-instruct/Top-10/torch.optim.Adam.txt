
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.optim.Adam`:

1. `tf.keras.optimizers.Adam`
2. `tf.train.AdamOptimizer`
3. `tf.contrib.opt.AdamOptimizer`
4. `tf.keras.optimizers.SGD` with momentum and Nesterov acceleration
5. `tf.train.GradientDescentOptimizer` with momentum and Nesterov acceleration
6. `tf.contrib.opt.MomentumOptimizer`
7. `tf.contrib.opt.NesterovAcceleratedGradientOptimizer`
8. `tf.keras.optimizers.RMSprop`
9. `tf.train.RMSPropOptimizer`
10. `tf.contrib.opt.RMSPropOptimizer`
