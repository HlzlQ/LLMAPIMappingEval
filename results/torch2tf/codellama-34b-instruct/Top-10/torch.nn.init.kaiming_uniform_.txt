
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for Kaiming uniform initialization:

1. `tf.keras.initializers.KaimingUniform()`
2. `tf.keras.initializers.HeUniform()`
3. `tf.keras.initializers.GlorotUniform()`
4. `tf.keras.initializers.VarianceScaling(scale=1, mode='fan_in', distribution='uniform')`
5. `tf.keras.initializers.TruncatedNormal(mean=0, stddev=1)`
6. `tf.keras.initializers.RandomUniform(minval=-1, maxval=1)`
7. `tf.keras.initializers.Orthogonal(gain=1)`
8. `tf.keras.initializers.Identity()`
9. `tf.keras.initializers.Constant(value=0)`
10. `tf.keras.initializers.Zeros()`
