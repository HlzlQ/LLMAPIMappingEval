
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.functional.sigmoid`:

1. `tf.keras.layers.Sigmoid`
2. `tf.keras.activations.sigmoid`
3. `tf.math.sigmoid`
4. `tf.nn.sigmoid_cross_entropy_with_logits`
5. `tf.nn.softmax_cross_entropy_with_logits`
6. `tf.keras.layers.Dense(units=1, activation='sigmoid')`
7. `tf.keras.layers.Sigmoid(name='sigmoid')`
8. `tf.nn.sigmoid`
9. `tf.keras.activations.softmax`
10. `tf.keras.layers.Softmax(units=1, activation='sigmoid')`
