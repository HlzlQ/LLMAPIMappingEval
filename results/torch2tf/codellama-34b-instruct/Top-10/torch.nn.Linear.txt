
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.Linear`:

1. `tf.keras.layers.Dense`
2. `tf.keras.layers.Flatten`
3. `tf.keras.layers.Reshape`
4. `tf.keras.layers.Conv2D`
5. `tf.keras.layers.MaxPooling2D`
6. `tf.keras.layers.AveragePooling2D`
7. `tf.keras.layers.GlobalAveragePooling2D`
8. `tf.keras.layers.GlobalMaxPooling2D`
9. `tf.keras.layers.BatchNormalization`
10. `tf.keras.layers.Dropout`
