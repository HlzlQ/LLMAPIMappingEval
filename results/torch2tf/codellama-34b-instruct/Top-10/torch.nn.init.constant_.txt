
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for initializing a constant tensor:

1. `tf.constant_initializer`
2. `tf.zeros_initializer`
3. `tf.ones_initializer`
4. `tf.fill_initializer`
5. `tf.random_normal_initializer`
6. `tf.truncated_normal_initializer`
7. `tf.variance_scaling_initializer`
8. `tf.glorot_uniform_initializer`
9. `tf.he_normal_initializer`
10. `tf.lecun_normal_initializer`
