
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used as alternatives to PyTorch's `torch.nn.CrossEntropyLoss`:

1. `tf.keras.losses.CategoricalCrossentropy`
2. `tf.keras.losses.SparseCategoricalCrossentropy`
3. `tf.keras.losses.BinaryCrossentropy`
4. `tf.keras.losses.KLDivergence`
5. `tf.keras.losses.MSE`
6. `tf.keras.losses.MAE`
7. `tf.keras.losses.Huber`
8. `tf.keras.losses.LogCosh`
9. `tf.keras.losses.Poisson`
10. `tf.keras.losses.KLU`
