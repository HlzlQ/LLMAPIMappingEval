
Here are ten possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for the same purpose as PyTorch's `torch.nn.init.zeros_`:

1. `tf.zeros`
2. `tf.zeros_like`
3. `tf.fill`
4. `tf.constant`
5. `tf.Variable` with a zero initializer
6. `tf.get_variable` with a zero initializer
7. `tf.placeholder` with a zero default value
8. `tf.zeros_initializer`
9. `tf.zeros_like_initializer`
10. `tf.fill_initializer`
