
Here are five possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for exponential learning rate scheduling:

1. `tf.keras.optimizers.schedulers.ExponentialDecay`
2. `tf.keras.optimizers.schedulers.CosineAnnealingLR`
3. `tf.keras.optimizers.schedulers.PolynomialDecay`
4. `tf.keras.optimizers.schedulers.InverseTimeDecay`
5. `tf.keras.optimizers.schedulers.NaturalExpDecay`
