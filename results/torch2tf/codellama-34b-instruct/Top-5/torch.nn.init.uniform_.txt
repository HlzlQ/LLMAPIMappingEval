
Here are five possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for uniform initialization:

1. `tf.initializers.uniform`
2. `tf.keras.initializers.Uniform`
3. `tf.keras.layers.Dense.kernel_initializer` with `distribution='uniform'`
4. `tf.keras.layers.Conv2D.kernel_initializer` with `distribution='uniform'`
5. `tf.keras.layers.Embedding.embeddings_initializer` with `distribution='uniform'`
