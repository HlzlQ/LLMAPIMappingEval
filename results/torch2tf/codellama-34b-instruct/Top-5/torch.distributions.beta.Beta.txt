
Here are five possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for the same purpose as PyTorch's `torch.distributions.beta.Beta`:

1. `tf.distributions.Beta`
2. `tf.distributions.BetaWithSoftplusConcentration`
3. `tf.distributions.BetaWithSoftplusRate`
4. `tf.distributions.BetaWithSoftplusConcentrationAndRate`
5. `tf.distributions.BetaWithSoftplusConcentrationAndRateLogits`
