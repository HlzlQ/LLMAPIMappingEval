
Here are five possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for Kaiming initialization:

1. `tf.keras.initializers.he_uniform()`
2. `tf.keras.initializers.glorot_uniform()`
3. `tf.keras.initializers.kaiming_uniform()`
4. `tf.keras.initializers.variance_scaling()`
5. `tf.keras.initializers.orthogonal()`
