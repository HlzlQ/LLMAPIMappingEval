
Here are five possible TensorFlow APIs that can be used for initializing weights with a normal distribution:

1. `tf.keras.initializers.RandomNormal`
2. `tf.keras.initializers.TruncatedNormal`
3. `tf.keras.initializers.VarianceScaling`
4. `tf.keras.initializers.GlorotUniform`
5. `tf.keras.initializers.HeNormal`
