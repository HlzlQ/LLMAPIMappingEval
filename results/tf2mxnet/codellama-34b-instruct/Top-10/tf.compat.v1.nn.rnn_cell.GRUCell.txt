
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.rnn_cell.GRUCell`:

1. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.GRUCell`
2. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.RNNCell`
3. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.LSTMCell`
4. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.GRU`
5. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.LSTM`
6. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.RNN`
7. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.SequentialRNNCell`
8. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.BidirectionalRNN`
9. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.StackedRNNCells`
10. `mxnet.gluon.rnn.MultipleInputsRNN`
