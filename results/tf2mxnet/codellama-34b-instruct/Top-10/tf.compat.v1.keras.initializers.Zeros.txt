
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.keras.initializers.Zeros`:

1. `mxnet.init.Zero`
2. `mxnet.init.Constant`
3. `mxnet.init.Ones`
4. `mxnet.init.RandomNormal`
5. `mxnet.init.RandomUniform`
6. `mxnet.init.TruncatedNormal`
7. `mxnet.init.VarianceScaling`
8. `mxnet.init.Orthogonal`
9. `mxnet.init.Identity`
10. `mxnet.init.Xavier`
