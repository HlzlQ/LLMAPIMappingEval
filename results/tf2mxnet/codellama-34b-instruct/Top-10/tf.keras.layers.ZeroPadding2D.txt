
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used for zero-padding a 2D tensor:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.pad`
2. `mxnet.ndarray.zero_pad`
3. `mxnet.ndarray.pad_zeros`
4. `mxnet.ndarray.pad_constant`
5. `mxnet.ndarray.pad_reflect`
6. `mxnet.ndarray.pad_replicate`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.pad_wrap`
8. `mxnet.ndarray.pad_edge`
9. `mxnet.ndarray.pad_linear_ramp`
10. `mxnet.ndarray.pad_constant_value`
