  Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that correspond to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.keras.losses.Huber`:

1. mxnet.gluon.loss.HuberLoss
2. mxnet.ndarray.huber_loss
3. mxnet.symbol.huber_loss
4. mxnet.gluon.loss.Huber
5. mxnet.ndarray.huber
6. mxnet.symbol.huber
7. mxnet.gluon.loss.SmoothL1Loss
8. mxnet.ndarray.smooth_l1_loss
9. mxnet.symbol.smooth_l1_loss
10. mxnet.gluon.loss.L1Loss

Note that some of these APIs may not be directly equivalent to the TensorFlow API, but they all implement a similar Huber loss function.
