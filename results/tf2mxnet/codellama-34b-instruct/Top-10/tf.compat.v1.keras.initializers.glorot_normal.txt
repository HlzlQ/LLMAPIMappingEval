
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `glorot_normal` initializer:

1. `mxnet.initializers.GlorotNormal()`
2. `mxnet.initializers.XavierNormal()`
3. `mxnet.initializers.HeNormal()`
4. `mxnet.initializers.LeCunNormal()`
5. `mxnet.initializers.KaimingNormal()`
6. `mxnet.initializers.VarianceScaling()`
7. `mxnet.initializers.RandomNormal()`
8. `mxnet.initializers.TruncatedNormal()`
9. `mxnet.initializers.Orthogonal()`
10. `mxnet.initializers.Identity()`
