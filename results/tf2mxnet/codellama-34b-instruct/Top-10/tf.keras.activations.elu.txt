  Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that correspond to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.keras.activations.elu`:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.ELU`
2. `mxnet.gluon.nn.ELU`
3. `mxnet.symbol.ELU`
4. `mxnet.ndarray.LeakyReLU`
5. `mxnet.gluon.nn.LeakyReLU`
6. `mxnet.symbol.LeakyReLU`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.PReLU`
8. `mxnet.gluon.nn.PReLU`
9. `mxnet.symbol.PReLU`
10. `mxnet.ndarray.RReLU`

Note that some of these APIs may not be exact replacements for the TensorFlow API, but they are similar and can be used in a similar way.
