
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.keras.initializers.Ones`:

1. `mxnet.init.Ones`
2. `mxnet.initializer.Ones`
3. `mxnet.initializers.ones`
4. `mxnet.init.Constant(1)`
5. `mxnet.initializer.Constant(1)`
6. `mxnet.initializers.constant(1)`
7. `mxnet.init.OnesLike`
8. `mxnet.initializer.OnesLike`
9. `mxnet.initializers.ones_like`
10. `mxnet.init.ConstantLike(1)`

Note that some of these APIs may not be exact replacements for TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.keras.initializers.Ones`, but they should all achieve the same goal of initializing a tensor with ones.
