
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used for loading a saved model:

1. `mxnet.model.load_checkpoint`
2. `mxnet.model.load_params`
3. `mxnet.model.load_state_dict`
4. `mxnet.model.load_weights`
5. `mxnet.model.load_optimizer`
6. `mxnet.model.load_scheduler`
7. `mxnet.model.load_loss`
8. `mxnet.model.load_metric`
9. `mxnet.model.load_callbacks`
10. `mxnet.model.load_model`
