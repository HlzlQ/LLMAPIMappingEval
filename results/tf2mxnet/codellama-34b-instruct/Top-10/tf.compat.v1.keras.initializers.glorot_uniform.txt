
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `glorot_uniform` initializer:

1. `mxnet.initializers.GlorotUniform()`
2. `mxnet.initializers.XavierUniform()`
3. `mxnet.initializers.HeUniform()`
4. `mxnet.initializers.LeCunUniform()`
5. `mxnet.initializers.KaimingUniform()`
6. `mxnet.initializers.VarianceScaling()`
7. `mxnet.initializers.RandomNormal()`
8. `mxnet.initializers.RandomUniform()`
9. `mxnet.initializers.TruncatedNormal()`
10. `mxnet.initializers.Orthogonal()`
