
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used for Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) with similar functionality to TensorFlow's `tensorflow.keras.layers.Conv1D`:

1. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv1D`
2. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Convolution1D`
3. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv1DTranspose`
4. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Convolution1DTranspose`
5. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv1DWithBias`
6. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Convolution1DWithBias`
7. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv1DWithoutBias`
8. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Convolution1DWithoutBias`
9. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv1DWithBatchNorm`
10. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Convolution1DWithBatchNorm`
