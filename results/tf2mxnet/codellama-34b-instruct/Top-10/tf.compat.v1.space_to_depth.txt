4. `mxnet.ndarray.transpose`
5. `mxnet.ndarray.permute`
6. `mxnet.ndarray.expand_dims`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.squeeze`
8. `mxnet.ndarray.reshape_like`
9. `mxnet.ndarray.broadcast_to`
