
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used for transposed convolutions:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.ConvolutionTranspose3D`
2. `mxnet.ndarray.ConvTranspose3D`
3. `mxnet.ndarray.Deconvolution3D`
4. `mxnet.ndarray.Deconv3D`
5. `mxnet.ndarray.ConvTranspose3DWithBias`
6. `mxnet.ndarray.ConvTranspose3DWithoutBias`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.Deconvolution3DWithBias`
8. `mxnet.ndarray.Deconv3DWithBias`
9. `mxnet.ndarray.ConvTranspose3DWithPadding`
10. `mxnet.ndarray.Deconvolution3DWithPadding`
