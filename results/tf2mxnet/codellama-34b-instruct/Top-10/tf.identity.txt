
Here are ten MXNet APIs that have similar functionality to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.identity`:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.identity`
2. `mxnet.symbol.identity`
3. `mxnet.tensor.identity`
4. `mxnet.ndarray.eye`
5. `mxnet.symbol.eye`
6. `mxnet.tensor.eye`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.ones_like`
8. `mxnet.symbol.ones_like`
9. `mxnet.tensor.ones_like`
10. `mxnet.ndarray.zeros_like`

Note that the MXNet APIs listed above are not exact replacements for the TensorFlow API, but they provide similar functionality and can be used to achieve similar results in MXNet.
