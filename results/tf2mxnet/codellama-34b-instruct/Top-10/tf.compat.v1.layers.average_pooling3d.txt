
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used for average pooling in 3D:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.Pooling`
2. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling_max`
3. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling_avg`
4. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling_sum`
5. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling_mean`
6. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling_std`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling_var`
8. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling_min`
9. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling_max_index`
10. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling_avg_index`
