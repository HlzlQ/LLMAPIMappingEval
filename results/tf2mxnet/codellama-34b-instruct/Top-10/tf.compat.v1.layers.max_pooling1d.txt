5. `mxnet.ndarray.max_pooling_1d_layer`
6. `mxnet.ndarray.MaxPooling1DCell`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.max_pooling_1d_cell`
8. `mxnet.ndarray.MaxPooling1DModule`
9. `mxnet.ndarray.max_pooling_1d_module`
10. `mxnet.ndarray.MaxPooling1DLayerNorm`
