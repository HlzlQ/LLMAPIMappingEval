
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that correspond to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.keras.losses.CosineSimilarity`:

1. `mxnet.gluon.loss.CosineSimilarity`
2. `mxnet.ndarray.cosine_similarity`
3. `mxnet.symbolic.cosine_similarity`
4. `mxnet.gluon.loss.CosineDistance`
5. `mxnet.ndarray.cosine_distance`
6. `mxnet.symbolic.cosine_distance`
7. `mxnet.gluon.loss.Similarity`
8. `mxnet.ndarray.similarity`
9. `mxnet.symbolic.similarity`
10. `mxnet.gluon.loss.Distance`

Note that some of these APIs may not be exact replacements for the TensorFlow API, but they should provide similar functionality.
