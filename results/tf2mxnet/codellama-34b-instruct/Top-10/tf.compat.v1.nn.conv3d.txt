
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used for 3D convolution:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.Convolution`
2. `mxnet.symbol.Convolution`
3. `mxnet.ndarray.Conv3D`
4. `mxnet.symbol.Conv3D`
5. `mxnet.ndarray.Conv3DTranspose`
6. `mxnet.symbol.Conv3DTranspose`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.Conv3DWithBias`
8. `mxnet.symbol.Conv3DWithBias`
9. `mxnet.ndarray.Conv3DWithoutBias`
10. `mxnet.symbol.Conv3DWithoutBias`
