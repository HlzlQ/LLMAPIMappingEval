
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `hinge_loss`:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.hinge_loss`
2. `mxnet.gluon.loss.HingeLoss`
3. `mxnet.symbol.hinge_loss`
4. `mxnet.ndarray.softmax_cross_entropy`
5. `mxnet.gluon.loss.SoftmaxCrossEntropyLoss`
6. `mxnet.symbol.softmax_cross_entropy`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.sparse_categorical_crossentropy`
8. `mxnet.gluon.loss.SparseCategoricalCrossEntropyLoss`
9. `mxnet.symbol.sparse_categorical_crossentropy`
10. `mxnet.ndarray.categorical_hinge_loss`
