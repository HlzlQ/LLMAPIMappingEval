
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used for convolutional neural networks (CNNs) with similar functionality to TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.conv1d`:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.Convolution`
2. `mxnet.symbol.Convolution`
3. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv1D`
4. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv2D`
5. `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv3D`
6. `mxnet.ndarray.convolution`
7. `mxnet.symbol.convolution`
8. `mxnet.gluon.nn.conv1d`
9. `mxnet.gluon.nn.conv2d`
10. `mxnet.gluon.nn.conv3d`
