
Here are five possible MXNet APIs that can be used for the same purpose as TensorFlow's `glorot_uniform` initializer:

1. `mxnet.initializers.GlorotUniform()`
2. `mxnet.initializers.XavierUniform()`
3. `mxnet.initializers.HeUniform()`
4. `mxnet.initializers.LeCunUniform()`
5. `mxnet.initializers.KaimingUniform()`
