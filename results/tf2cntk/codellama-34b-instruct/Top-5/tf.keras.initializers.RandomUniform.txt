
Here are five possible CNTK APIs that correspond to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.keras.initializers.RandomUniform`:

1. `cntk.initializer.uniform`
2. `cntk.initializer.random_uniform`
3. `cntk.initializer.uniform_distribution`
4. `cntk.initializer.random_normal`
5. `cntk.initializer.truncated_normal`
