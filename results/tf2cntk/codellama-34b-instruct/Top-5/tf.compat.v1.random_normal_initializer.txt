
Here are five possible CNTK APIs that can be used as alternatives to `tensorflow.compat.v1.random_normal_initializer`:

1. `cntk.initializer.RandomNormalInitializer`
2. `cntk.initializer.TruncatedNormalInitializer`
3. `cntk.initializer.UniformInitializer`
4. `cntk.initializer.GlorotNormalInitializer`
5. `cntk.initializer.HeNormalInitializer`
