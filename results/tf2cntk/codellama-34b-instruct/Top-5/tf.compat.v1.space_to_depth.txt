
Here are five possible CNTK APIs that can be used for space-to-depth conversion:

1. `cntk.layers.ConvolutionalLayer` with `convolution_type='space_to_depth'`
2. `cntk.layers.DepthwiseConvolutionalLayer` with `convolution_type='space_to_depth'`
3. `cntk.layers.SeparableConvolutionalLayer` with `convolution_type='space_to_depth'`
4. `cntk.layers.ConvolutionalTransposeLayer` with `convolution_type='space_to_depth'`
5. `cntk.layers.DepthwiseDeconvolutionalLayer` with `convolution_type='space_to_depth'`
