
Here are five possible CNTK APIs that can be used as alternatives to `tensorflow.random_uniform_initializer`:

1. `cntk.initializer.UniformInitializer`
2. `cntk.initializer.RandomNormalInitializer`
3. `cntk.initializer.TruncatedNormalInitializer`
4. `cntk.initializer.XavierInitializer`
5. `cntk.initializer.HeInitializer`
