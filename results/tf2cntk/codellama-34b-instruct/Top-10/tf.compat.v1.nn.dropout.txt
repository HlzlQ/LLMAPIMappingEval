
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.dropout`:

1. `cntk.layers.Dropout`
2. `cntk.ops.dropout`
3. `cntk.layers.dropout_layer`
4. `cntk.layers.dropout_op`
5. `cntk.ops.dropout_op`
6. `cntk.layers.dropout_function`
7. `cntk.ops.dropout_function`
8. `cntk.layers.dropout_layer_with_mask`
9. `cntk.ops.dropout_layer_with_mask`
10. `cntk.layers.dropout_op_with_mask`
