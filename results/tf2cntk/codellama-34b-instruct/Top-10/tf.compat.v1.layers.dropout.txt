1. `cntk.layers.Dropout`
2. `cntk.ops.dropout`
3. `cntk.layers.dropout_layer`
4. `cntk.layers.dropout_op`
5. `cntk.ops.dropout_layer`
6. `cntk.ops.dropout_op`
7. `cntk.layers.dropout_function`
8. `cntk.ops.dropout_function`
9. `cntk.layers.dropout_block`
10. `cntk.ops.dropout_block`
