
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that can be used for loading a saved model:

1. `cntk.load_model`
2. `cntk.load_model_from_file`
3. `cntk.load_model_from_string`
4. `cntk.load_model_from_bytes`
5. `cntk.load_model_from_url`
6. `cntk.load_model_from_stream`
7. `cntk.load_model_from_memory`
8. `cntk.load_model_from_disk`
9. `cntk.load_model_from_database`
10. `cntk.load_model_from_cloud`
