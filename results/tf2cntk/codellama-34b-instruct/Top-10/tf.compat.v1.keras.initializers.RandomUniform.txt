
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.keras.initializers.RandomUniform`:

1. `cntk.initializer.uniform`
2. `cntk.initializer.random_uniform`
3. `cntk.initializer.glorot_normal`
4. `cntk.initializer.glorot_uniform`
5. `cntk.initializer.he_normal`
6. `cntk.initializer.he_uniform`
7. `cntk.initializer.lecun_normal`
8. `cntk.initializer.lecun_uniform`
9. `cntk.initializer.kaiming_normal`
10. `cntk.initializer.kaiming_uniform`
