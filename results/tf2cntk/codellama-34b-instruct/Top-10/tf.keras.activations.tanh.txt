5. `cntk.layers.Conv1D(activation=cntk.ops.tanh)`
6. `cntk.layers.Conv2D(activation=cntk.ops.tanh)`
7. `cntk.layers.Conv3D(activation=cntk.ops.tanh)`
8. `cntk.layers.LSTM(activation=cntk.ops.tanh)`
9. `cntk.layers.GRU(activation=cntk.ops.tanh)`
10. `cntk.layers.RNN(activation=cntk.ops.tanh)`
