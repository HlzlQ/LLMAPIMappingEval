4. `cntk.layers.max_pooling3d_with_padding`
5. `cntk.layers.MaxPooling3DWithStride`
6. `cntk.layers.max_pooling3d_with_stride`
7. `cntk.layers.MaxPooling3DWithKernelSize`
8. `cntk.layers.max_pooling3d_with_kernel_size`
9. `cntk.layers.MaxPooling3DWithStrideAndPadding`
10. `cntk.layers.max_pooling3d_with_stride_and_padding`
