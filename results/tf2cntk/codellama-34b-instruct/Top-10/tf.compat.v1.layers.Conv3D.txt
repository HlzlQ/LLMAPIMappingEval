
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that can be used for Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) with 3D data:

1. `cntk.layers.Convolution3D`
2. `cntk.layers.Conv3D`
3. `cntk.layers.ConvolutionTranspose3D`
4. `cntk.layers.Deconvolution3D`
5. `cntk.layers.MaxPooling3D`
6. `cntk.layers.AveragePooling3D`
7. `cntk.layers.GlobalMaxPooling3D`
8. `cntk.layers.GlobalAveragePooling3D`
9. `cntk.layers.BatchNormalization3D`
10. `cntk.layers.InstanceNormalization3D`
