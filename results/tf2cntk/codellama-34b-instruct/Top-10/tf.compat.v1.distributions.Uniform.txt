
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that can be used for the same functionality as TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.distributions.Uniform`:

1. `cntk.uniform`
2. `cntk.random.uniform`
3. `cntk.distributions.uniform`
4. `cntk.random.normal`
5. `cntk.distributions.normal`
6. `cntk.random.truncated_normal`
7. `cntk.distributions.truncated_normal`
8. `cntk.random.logistic`
9. `cntk.distributions.logistic`
10. `cntk.random.exponential`
