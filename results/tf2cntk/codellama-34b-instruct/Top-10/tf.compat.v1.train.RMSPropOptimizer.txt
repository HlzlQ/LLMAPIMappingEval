
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `RMSPropOptimizer`:

1. `cntk.learners.rmsprop`
2. `cntk.optimizers.rmsprop`
3. `cntk.trainers.rmsprop`
4. `cntk.learners.adam`
5. `cntk.optimizers.adam`
6. `cntk.trainers.adam`
7. `cntk.learners.sgd`
8. `cntk.optimizers.sgd`
9. `cntk.trainers.sgd`
10. `cntk.learners.momentum_sgd`
