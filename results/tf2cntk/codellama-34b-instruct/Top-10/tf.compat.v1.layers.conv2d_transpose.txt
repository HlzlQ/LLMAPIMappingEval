1. `cntk.layers.ConvTranspose2D`
2. `cntk.layers.Deconv2D`
3. `cntk.layers.ConvTranspose3D`
4. `cntk.layers.Deconv3D`
5. `cntk.layers.ConvTranspose1D`
6. `cntk.layers.Deconv1D`
7. `cntk.layers.ConvTranspose2DWithBias`
8. `cntk.layers.Deconv2DWithBias`
9. `cntk.layers.ConvTranspose3DWithBias`
10. `cntk.layers.Deconv3DWithBias`
