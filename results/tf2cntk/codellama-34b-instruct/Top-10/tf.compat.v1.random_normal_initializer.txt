4. `CNTK.initializers.LeCunNormal`
5. `CNTK.initializers.XavierNormal`
6. `CNTK.initializers.MSRANormal`
7. `CNTK.initializers.RandomUniform`
8. `CNTK.initializers.GlorotUniform`
9. `CNTK.initializers.HeUniform`
10. `CNTK.initializers.LeCunUniform`
