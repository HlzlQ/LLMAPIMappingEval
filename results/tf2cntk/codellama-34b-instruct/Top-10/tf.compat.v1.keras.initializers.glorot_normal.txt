
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `glorot_normal` initializer:

1. `CNTK.initializers.GlorotNormal()`
2. `CNTK.initializers.XavierNormal()`
3. `CNTK.initializers.HeNormal()`
4. `CNTK.initializers.LeCunNormal()`
5. `CNTK.initializers.MSRANormal()`
6. `CNTK.initializers.RandomNormal()`
7. `CNTK.initializers.TruncatedNormal()`
8. `CNTK.initializers.VarianceScaling()`
9. `CNTK.initializers.Orthogonal()`
10. `CNTK.initializers.Identity()`
