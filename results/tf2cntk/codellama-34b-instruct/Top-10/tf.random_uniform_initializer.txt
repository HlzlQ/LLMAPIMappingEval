
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that can be used as alternatives to TensorFlow's `random_uniform_initializer`:

1. `CNTK.initializers.RandomUniform`
2. `CNTK.initializers.Uniform`
3. `CNTK.initializers.GlorotUniform`
4. `CNTK.initializers.HeUniform`
5. `CNTK.initializers.LeCunUniform`
6. `CNTK.initializers.XavierUniform`
7. `CNTK.initializers.MSRAUniform`
8. `CNTK.initializers.RandomNormal`
9. `CNTK.initializers.TruncatedNormal`
10. `CNTK.initializers.Orthogonal`
