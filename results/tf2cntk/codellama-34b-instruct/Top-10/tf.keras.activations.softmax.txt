
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that correspond to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.keras.activations.softmax`:

1. `cntk.layers.Softmax`
2. `cntk.layers.Activation(cntk.ops.softmax)`
3. `cntk.layers.Dense(cntk.ops.softmax)`
4. `cntk.layers.Activation(cntk.ops.softmax, cntk.ops.softmax_cross_entropy)`
5. `cntk.layers.SoftmaxCrossEntropy`
6. `cntk.layers.SoftmaxLoss`
7. `cntk.layers.SoftmaxClassifier`
8. `cntk.layers.SoftmaxRegression`
9. `cntk.layers.SoftmaxBinaryClassification`
10. `cntk.layers.SoftmaxMultiClassClassification`
