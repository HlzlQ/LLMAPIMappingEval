  Here are ten Java methods that correspond to the C# `char.IsControl` method:

1. `Character.isISOControl(char ch)`
2. `Character.isWhitespace(char ch)`
3. `Character.isSpaceChar(char ch)`
4. `Character.isWhitespaceOrControl(char ch)`
5. `Character.isControl(char ch)`
6. `Character.isDefined(char ch)`
7. `Character.isDigit(char ch)`
8. `Character.isLetter(char ch)`
9. `Character.isLetterOrDigit(char ch)`
10. `Character.isJavaIdentifierStart(char ch)`
