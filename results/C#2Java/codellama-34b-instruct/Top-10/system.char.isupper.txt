1. `Character.isUpperCase(char ch)`
2. `String.toUpperCase()`
3. `StringBuilder.toString().toUpperCase()`
4. `StringBuffer.toString().toUpperCase()`
5. `CharSequence.toString().toUpperCase()`
6. `TextUtils.getTrimmedLength(CharSequence s)`
7. `TextUtils.isEmpty(CharSequence s)`
8. `TextUtils.isDigitsOnly(CharSequence s)`
9. `TextUtils.getReverse(CharSequence s, int start, int end)`
10. `TextUtils.join(CharSequence delimiter, Iterable<? extends CharSequence> elements)`
