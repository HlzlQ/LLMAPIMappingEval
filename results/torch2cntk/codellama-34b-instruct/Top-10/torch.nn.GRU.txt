
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that correspond to the PyTorch `torch.nn.GRU` API:

1. `cntk.layers.RecurrentBlock`
2. `cntk.layers.LSTM`
3. `cntk.layers.GRU`
4. `cntk.layers.RNN`
5. `cntk.layers.Sequential`
6. `cntk.layers.StackedRNN`
7. `cntk.layers.BidirectionalRNN`
8. `cntk.layers.GRUCell`
9. `cntk.layers.LSTMCell`
10. `cntk.layers.RNNCell`
