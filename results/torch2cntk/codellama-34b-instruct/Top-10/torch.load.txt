
Here are ten possible mappings from the `torch.load` API to the CNTK API:

1. `torch.load` -> `CNTK.LoadModel`
2. `torch.load_state_dict` -> `CNTK.LoadStateDict`
3. `torch.save` -> `CNTK.SaveModel`
4. `torch.save_state_dict` -> `CNTK.SaveStateDict`
5. `torch.load_parameters` -> `CNTK.LoadParameters`
6. `torch.save_parameters` -> `CNTK.SaveParameters`
7. `torch.load_optimizer` -> `CNTK.LoadOptimizer`
8. `torch.save_optimizer` -> `CNTK.SaveOptimizer`
9. `torch.load_scheduler` -> `CNTK.LoadScheduler`
10. `torch.save_scheduler` -> `CNTK.SaveScheduler`
