1. `cntk.learners.sgd`
2. `cntk.trainers.sgd`
3. `cntk.optimizers.sgd`
4. `cntk.learners.stochastic_gradient_descent`
5. `cntk.trainers.stochastic_gradient_descent`
6. `cntk.optimizers.stochastic_gradient_descent`
7. `cntk.learners.sgd_momentum`
8. `cntk.trainers.sgd_momentum`
9. `cntk.optimizers.sgd_momentum`
10. `cntk.learners.stochastic_gradient_descent_with_momentum`
