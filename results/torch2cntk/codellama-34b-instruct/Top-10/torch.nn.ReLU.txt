
Here are ten possible CNTK APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.nn.ReLU`:

1. `cntk.layers.Activation(activation='relu')`
2. `cntk.layers.ReLU()`
3. `cntk.layers.LeakyReLU()`
4. `cntk.layers.ParametricReLU()`
5. `cntk.layers.PReLU()`
6. `cntk.layers.SoftReLU()`
7. `cntk.layers.SmoothReLU()`
8. `cntk.layers.Swish()`
9. `cntk.layers.Mish()`
10. `cntk.layers.HardSwish()`
