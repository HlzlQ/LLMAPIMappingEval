5. `cntk.layers.dropout_module`
6. `cntk.layers.dropout_op`
7. `cntk.layers.dropout_layer_norm`
8. `cntk.layers.dropout_batch_norm`
9. `cntk.layers.dropout_instance_norm`
10. `cntk.layers.dropout_group_norm`
