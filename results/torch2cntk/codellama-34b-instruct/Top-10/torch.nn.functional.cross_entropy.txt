1. `cntk.losses.cross_entropy`
2. `cntk.layers.CrossEntropyLoss`
3. `cntk.objectives.cross_entropy`
4. `cntk.metrics.cross_entropy`
5. `cntk.train.cross_entropy`
6. `cntk.losses.softmax_cross_entropy`
7. `cntk.layers.SoftmaxCrossEntropyLoss`
8. `cntk.objectives.softmax_cross_entropy`
9. `cntk.metrics.softmax_cross_entropy`
10. `cntk.train.softmax_cross_entropy`
