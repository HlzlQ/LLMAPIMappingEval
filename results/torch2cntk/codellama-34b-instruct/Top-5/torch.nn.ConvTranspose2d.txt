
Here are five possible CNTK APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.nn.ConvTranspose2d`:

1. `cntk.layers.ConvolutionTranspose2D`
2. `cntk.layers.Deconvolution2D`
3. `cntk.layers.UpSampling2D`
4. `cntk.layers.ConvolutionalLayer` with a transposed convolution kernel
5. `cntk.layers.DeconvolutionalLayer`
