
Here are five possible CNTK APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.nn.Softplus`:

1. `cntk.layers.Softplus`
2. `cntk.activations.softplus`
3. `cntk.layers.Activation(cntk.activations.softplus)`
4. `cntk.layers.Dense(cntk.activations.softplus)`
5. `cntk.layers.Conv2D(cntk.activations.softplus)`
