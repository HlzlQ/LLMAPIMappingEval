
Here are five possible CNTK APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.nn.functional.binary_cross_entropy`:

1. `cntk.losses.BinaryCrossEntropy`
2. `cntk.losses.CrossEntropyWithSoftmax`
3. `cntk.losses.SigmoidCrossEntropy`
4. `cntk.losses.SoftmaxCrossEntropy`
5. `cntk.losses.BinaryCrossEntropyLoss`
