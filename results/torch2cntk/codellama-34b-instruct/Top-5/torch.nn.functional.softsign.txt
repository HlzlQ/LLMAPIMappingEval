
Here are five possible CNTK APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.nn.functional.softsign`:

1. `cntk.ops.softsign`
2. `cntk.layers.activation.Softsign`
3. `cntk.layers.activation.Softmax` (with `axis=0`)
4. `cntk.layers.activation.Sigmoid` (with `axis=0`)
5. `cntk.layers.activation.Tanh` (with `axis=0`)
