

## Solution

1. `torch.nn.functional.relu` -> `mxnet.ndarray.relu`
2. `torch.nn.functional.sigmoid` -> `mxnet.ndarray.sigmoid`
3. `torch.nn.functional.softmax` -> `mxnet.ndarray.softmax`
4. `torch.nn.functional.log_softmax` -> `mxnet.ndarray.log_softmax`
5. `torch.nn.functional.cross_entropy` -> `mxnet.ndarray.softmax_cross_entropy`
6. `torch.nn.functional.binary_cross_entropy` -> `mxnet.ndarray.sigmoid_cross_entropy`
