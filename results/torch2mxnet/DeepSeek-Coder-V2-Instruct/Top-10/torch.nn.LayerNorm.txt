

## Solution:

Here are the 10 most likely MXNet APIs that have similar functionality to `torch.nn.LayerNorm`:

1. **mxnet.gluon.nn.LayerNorm**
2. **mxnet.ndarray.L2Normalization** (Note: This is more for normalization across batch and features, but still relevant)
3. **mxnet.ndarray.BatchNorm**
4. **mxnet.ndarray.InstanceNorm**
5. **mxnet.ndarray.GroupNorm**
6. **mxnet.ndarray.SyncBatchNorm**
7. **mxnet.gluon.nn.BatchNorm**
8. **mxnet.gluon.nn.InstanceNorm**
9. **mxnet.gluon.nn.GroupNorm**
10. **mxnet.gluon.nn.SyncBatchNorm**
