

## 解题答案

以下是与PyTorch中的`torch.nn.Upsample`功能最接近的MXNet API：

1. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.BilinearResize2D`
2. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.TriangularCubicResize2D`
3. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.PixelShuffle`
4. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.DeformableConvolution`
5. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.SpatialTransformer`
6. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.Upsampling`
7. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.BicubicResize2D`
8. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.DeformableConvolutionV2`
9. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.SpatialTransformerV2`
10. `mxnet.ndarray.contrib.PixelUnshuffle`
