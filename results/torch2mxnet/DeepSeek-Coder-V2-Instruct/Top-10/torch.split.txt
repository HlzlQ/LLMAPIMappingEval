

## Solution

1. `torch.cat` -> `mxnet.ndarray.concat`
2. `torch.stack` -> `mxnet.ndarray.stack`
3. `torch.chunk` -> `mxnet.ndarray.split`
4. `torch.split` -> `mxnet.ndarray.split`
5. `torch.unsqueeze` -> `mxnet.ndarray.expand_dims`
6. `torch.squeeze` -> `mxnet.ndarray.squeeze`
7. `torch.transpose` -> `mxnet.ndarray.transpose`
8. `torch.permute` -> `mxnet.ndarray.transpose`
9. `torch.reshape` -> `mxnet.ndarray.reshape`
10. `torch.view` -> `mxnet.ndarray.reshape`
