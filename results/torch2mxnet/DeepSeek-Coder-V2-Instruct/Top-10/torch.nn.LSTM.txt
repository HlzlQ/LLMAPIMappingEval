

## Solution

1. `torch.nn.Linear` -> `mxnet.gluon.nn.Dense`
2. `torch.nn.Conv1d` -> `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv1D`
3. `torch.nn.Conv2d` -> `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv2D`
4. `torch.nn.ConvTranspose1d` -> `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv1DTranspose`
5. `torch.nn.ConvTranspose2d` -> `mxnet.gluon.nn.Conv2DTranspose`
6. `torch.nn.BatchNorm1d` -> `mxnet.gluon.nn.BatchNorm`
7. `torch.nn.BatchNorm2d` -> `mxnet.gluon.nn.BatchNorm`
8. `torch.nn.Dropout` -> `mxnet.gluon.nn.Dropout`
9. `torch.nn.LSTM` -> `mxnet.gluon.rnn.LSTM`
10. `torch.nn.GRU` -> `mxnet.gluon.rnn.GRU`
