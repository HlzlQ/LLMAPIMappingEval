  Here are five possible MXNet APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.nn.functional.hardsigmoid`:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.hard_sigmoid`
2. `mxnet.ndarray.hard_sigmoid_elementwise`
3. `mxnet.ndarray.hard_sigmoid_channelwise`
4. `mxnet.ndarray.hard_sigmoid_spatial`
5. `mxnet.ndarray.hard_sigmoid_depthwise`
