  Here are five possible MXNet APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.nn.init.xavier_uniform`:

1. `mxnet.initializer.Xavier()`
2. `mxnet.initializer.XavierUniform()`
3. `mxnet.initializer.VarianceScaling(scale=1, mode='fan_avg', distribution='uniform')`
4. `mxnet.initializer.GlorotUniform()`
5. `mxnet.initializer.HeUniform()`
