  Here are five possible MXNet APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.nn.functional.cross_entropy`:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.softmax_cross_entropy`
2. `mxnet.ndarray.sparse_softmax_cross_entropy`
3. `mxnet.ndarray.log_softmax_cross_entropy`
4. `mxnet.ndarray.sparse_log_softmax_cross_entropy`
5. `mxnet.gluon.loss.SoftmaxCrossEntropyLoss`
