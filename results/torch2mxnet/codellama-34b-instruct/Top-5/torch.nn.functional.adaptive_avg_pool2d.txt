
Here are five possible MXNet APIs that can be used for adaptive average pooling:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.adaptive_avg_pool2d`
2. `mxnet.symbol.adaptive_avg_pool2d`
3. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling.adaptive_avg_pool2d`
4. `mxnet.symbol.pooling.adaptive_avg_pool2d`
5. `mxnet.ndarray.pooling.adaptive_avg_pooling`
