  The following are ten MXNet APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.einsum`:

1. `mxnet.ndarray.einsum`
2. `mxnet.numpy.einsum`
3. `mxnet.tensor.einsum`
4. `mxnet.linalg.einsum`
5. `mxnet.math.einsum`
6. `mxnet.array_ops.einsum`
7. `mxnet.matrix_ops.einsum`
8. `mxnet.tensor_ops.einsum`
9. `mxnet.linalg.tensor_einsum`
10. `mxnet.math.tensor_einsum`
