
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used for Xavier normal initialization:

1. `mxnet.initializer.Xavier()`
2. `mxnet.initializer.xavier_normal()`
3. `mxnet.initializer.xavier_uniform()`
4. `mxnet.initializer.xavier_normal_init()`
5. `mxnet.initializer.xavier_uniform_init()`
6. `mxnet.initializer.XavierNormal()`
7. `mxnet.initializer.XavierUniform()`
8. `mxnet.initializer.xavier_normal_init_()`
9. `mxnet.initializer.xavier_uniform_init_()`
10. `mxnet.initializer.XavierNormalInit()`

Note that the exact API names and parameters may vary depending on the version of MXNet you are using.
