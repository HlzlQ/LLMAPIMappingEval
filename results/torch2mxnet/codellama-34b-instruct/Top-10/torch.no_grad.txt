
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that correspond to the PyTorch API `torch.no_grad`:

1. `mxnet.autograd.no_grad()`
2. `mxnet.autograd.disable_gradients()`
3. `mxnet.autograd.set_gradients(False)`
4. `mxnet.autograd.freeze_gradients()`
5. `mxnet.autograd.stop_gradient()`
6. `mxnet.autograd.disable_backprop()`
7. `mxnet.autograd.set_backprop(False)`
8. `mxnet.autograd.freeze_backprop()`
9. `mxnet.autograd.stop_backprop()`
10. `mxnet.autograd.disable_gradients_and_backprop()`
