
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that can be used for saving models:

1. `mxnet.save_checkpoint`
2. `mxnet.save_parameters`
3. `mxnet.save_model`
4. `mxnet.save_state_dict`
5. `mxnet.save_weights`
6. `mxnet.save_optimizer`
7. `mxnet.save_lr_scheduler`
8. `mxnet.save_loss`
9. `mxnet.save_accuracy`
10. `mxnet.save_metrics`
