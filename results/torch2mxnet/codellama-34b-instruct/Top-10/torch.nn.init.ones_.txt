
Here are ten possible MXNet APIs that correspond to `torch.nn.init.ones_`:

1. `mxnet.initializer.Ones()`
2. `mxnet.initializer.Constant(1)`
3. `mxnet.initializer.Zeros()`
4. `mxnet.initializer.RandomUniform(0, 1)`
5. `mxnet.initializer.RandomNormal(0, 1)`
6. `mxnet.initializer.TruncatedNormal(0, 1)`
7. `mxnet.initializer.VarianceScaling(scale=1, mode='fan_in')`
8. `mxnet.initializer.GlorotUniform()`
9. `mxnet.initializer.HeNormal()`
10. `mxnet.initializer.LeCunUniform()`
