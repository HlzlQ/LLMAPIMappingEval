  Here are ten C# APIs that have similar functionality to the Java `String.toLowerCase()` method:

1. `string.ToLower()`
2. `string.ToLowerInvariant()`
3. `string.ToLower(CultureInfo.CurrentCulture)`
4. `string.ToLower(CultureInfo.CurrentUICulture)`
5. `string.ToLower(CultureInfo.InvariantCulture)`
6. `string.ToLower(CultureInfo.InstalledUICulture)`
7. `string.ToLower(CultureInfo.DefaultThreadCurrentCulture)`
8. `string.ToLower(CultureInfo.DefaultThreadCurrentUICulture)`
9. `string.ToLower(CultureInfo.GetCultureInfo("en-US"))`
10. `string.ToLower(CultureInfo.GetCultureInfo("en-GB"))`
