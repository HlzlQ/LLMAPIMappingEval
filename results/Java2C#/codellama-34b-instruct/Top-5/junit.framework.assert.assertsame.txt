JUnit is a unit testing framework for Java, and it provides several assertions to verify the behavior of code. In C#, there are several alternatives to JUnit assertions, but here are five APIs that provide similar functionality:

1. NUnit.Framework.Assert.AreSame
2. Microsoft.VisualStudio.TestTools.UnitTesting.Assert.AreSame
3. xunit.assert.same
4. FluentAssertions.AssertionExtensions.ShouldBeSameAs
5. ApprovalTests.Approvals.VerifySame
