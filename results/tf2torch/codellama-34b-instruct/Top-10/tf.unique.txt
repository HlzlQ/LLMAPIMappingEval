
Here are ten PyTorch APIs that correspond to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.unique`:

1. `torch.unique`
2. `torch.unique_dim`
3. `torch.unique_rows`
4. `torch.unique_cols`
5. `torch.unique_values`
6. `torch.unique_indices`
7. `torch.unique_counts`
8. `torch.unique_inverse`
9. `torch.unique_mask`
10. `torch.unique_scatter`
