
Here are ten possible PyTorch APIs that can be used for convolutional neural networks (CNNs) with similar functionality to TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.conv1d`:

1. `torch.nn.Conv1d`
2. `torch.nn.ConvTranspose1d`
3. `torch.nn.Conv2d`
4. `torch.nn.ConvTranspose2d`
5. `torch.nn.Conv3d`
6. `torch.nn.ConvTranspose3d`
7. `torch.nn.functional.conv1d`
8. `torch.nn.functional.conv_transpose1d`
9. `torch.nn.functional.conv2d`
10. `torch.nn.functional.conv_transpose2d`
