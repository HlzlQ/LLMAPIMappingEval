
Here are ten PyTorch APIs that can be used for max pooling in one dimension:

1. torch.nn.MaxPool1d
2. torch.nn.functional.max_pool1d
3. torch.nn.modules.pooling.MaxPool1d
4. torch.nn.modules.pooling.MaxPool1dWithIndices
5. torch.nn.modules.pooling.MaxUnpool1d
6. torch.nn.modules.pooling.MaxUnpool1dWithIndices
7. torch.nn.functional.max_unpool1d
8. torch.nn.functional.max_unpool1d_with_indices
9. torch.nn.modules.pooling.MaxPool1dWithMask
10. torch.nn.modules.pooling.MaxUnpool1dWithMask
