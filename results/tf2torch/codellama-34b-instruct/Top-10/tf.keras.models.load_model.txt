
Here are ten possible PyTorch APIs that can be used for loading a pre-trained model:

1. `torch.load()`
2. `torch.jit.load()`
3. `torch.nn.Module.load_state_dict()`
4. `torch.nn.Module.load_parameters()`
5. `torch.nn.Module.load_weights()`
6. `torch.nn.Module.load_model()`
7. `torch.nn.Module.load_from_checkpoint()`
8. `torch.nn.Module.load_from_pretrained()`
9. `torch.nn.Module.load_from_state_dict()`
10. `torch.nn.Module.load_from_parameters()`
