
Here are ten PyTorch APIs that correspond to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.keras.activations.sigmoid`:

1. `torch.nn.Sigmoid`
2. `torch.nn.functional.sigmoid`
3. `torch.nn.modules.activation.Sigmoid`
4. `torch.nn.modules.activation.ReLU` (with a negative slope)
5. `torch.nn.modules.activation.LeakyReLU` (with a negative slope)
6. `torch.nn.modules.activation.Softmax`
7. `torch.nn.modules.activation.LogSoftmax`
8. `torch.nn.modules.activation.Tanh`
9. `torch.nn.modules.activation.Hardtanh`
10. `torch.nn.modules.activation.SigmoidWithDropout`
