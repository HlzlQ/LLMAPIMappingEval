
Here are ten possible PyTorch APIs that can be used for the same purpose as TensorFlow's `he_normal` initializer:

1. `torch.nn.init.kaiming_uniform_`
2. `torch.nn.init.kaiming_normal_`
3. `torch.nn.init.xavier_uniform_`
4. `torch.nn.init.xavier_normal_`
5. `torch.nn.init.he_uniform_`
6. `torch.nn.init.he_normal_`
7. `torch.nn.init.lecun_uniform_`
8. `torch.nn.init.lecun_normal_`
9. `torch.nn.init.glorot_uniform_`
10. `torch.nn.init.glorot_normal_`
