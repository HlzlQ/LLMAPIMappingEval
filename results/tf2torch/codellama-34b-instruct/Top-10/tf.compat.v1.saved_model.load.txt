
Here are ten possible PyTorch APIs that can be used for loading a saved model:

1. `torch.load()`
2. `torch.jit.load()`
3. `torch.nn.Module.load_state_dict()`
4. `torch.nn.Module.load_parameters()`
5. `torch.optim.Optimizer.load_state_dict()`
6. `torch.utils.data.DataLoader.load_state_dict()`
7. `torch.nn.parallel.DistributedDataParallel.load_state_dict()`
8. `torch.nn.parallel.DistributedDataParallel.load_parameters()`
9. `torch.nn.utils.rnn.PackedSequence.load_state_dict()`
10. `torch.nn.utils.rnn.PackedSequence.load_parameters()`
