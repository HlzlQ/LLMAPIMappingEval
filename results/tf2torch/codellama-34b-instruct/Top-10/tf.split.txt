
Here are ten PyTorch APIs that correspond to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.split`:

1. `torch.split`
2. `torch.chunk`
3. `torch.tensor_split`
4. `torch.array_split`
5. `torch.split_dimension`
6. `torch.split_axis`
7. `torch.split_along_axis`
8. `torch.split_by_size`
9. `torch.split_by_length`
10. `torch.split_by_value`
