
Here are ten possible PyTorch APIs that can be used for the same purpose as TensorFlow's `he_uniform` initializer:

1. `torch.nn.init.kaiming_uniform_`
2. `torch.nn.init.xavier_uniform_`
3. `torch.nn.init.normal_`
4. `torch.nn.init.truncated_normal_`
5. `torch.nn.init.variance_scaling_`
6. `torch.nn.init.glorot_uniform_`
7. `torch.nn.init.he_uniform_`
8. `torch.nn.init.lecun_uniform_`
9. `torch.nn.init.orthogonal_`
10. `torch.nn.init.sparse_`
