4. `torch.autograd.grad_outputs`
5. `torch.autograd.grad_params`
6. `torch.autograd.grad_weights`
7. `torch.autograd.grad_biases`
8. `torch.autograd.grad_activations`
9. `torch.autograd.grad_derivatives`
10. `torch.autograd.grad_hessians`
