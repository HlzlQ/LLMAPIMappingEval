
Here are ten possible PyTorch APIs that can be used for convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and their corresponding TensorFlow API:

1. `torch.nn.Conv2d` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.conv2d`
2. `torch.nn.MaxPool2d` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.max_pool2d`
3. `torch.nn.AvgPool2d` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.avg_pool2d`
4. `torch.nn.BatchNorm2d` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.batch_normalization`
5. `torch.nn.ReLU` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.relu`
6. `torch.nn.LeakyReLU` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.leaky_relu`
7. `torch.nn.Sigmoid` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.sigmoid`
8. `torch.nn.Tanh` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.tanh`
9. `torch.nn.Softmax` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.softmax`
10. `torch.nn.Dropout` - `tensorflow.compat.v1.nn.dropout`
