
Here are ten PyTorch APIs that can be used for upsampling in a similar way to TensorFlow's `tensorflow.keras.layers.UpSampling2D`:

1. `torch.nn.Upsample`
2. `torch.nn.functional.interpolate`
3. `torch.nn.functional.upsample_bilinear`
4. `torch.nn.functional.upsample_nearest`
5. `torch.nn.functional.upsample_bicubic`
6. `torch.nn.functional.upsample_trilinear`
7. `torch.nn.functional.upsample_trilinear_3d`
8. `torch.nn.functional.upsample_nearest_3d`
9. `torch.nn.functional.upsample_bilinear_3d`
10. `torch.nn.functional.upsample_bicubic_3d`
