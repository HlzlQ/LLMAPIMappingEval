
Here are five possible PyTorch APIs that can be used for transposed convolutions in 3D:

1. `torch.nn.ConvTranspose3d`
2. `torch.nn.functional.conv_transpose3d`
3. `torch.nn.modules.conv.ConvTranspose3d`
4. `torch.nn.modules.conv.ConvTranspose3dModule`
5. `torch.nn.modules.conv.ConvTranspose3dLayer`
