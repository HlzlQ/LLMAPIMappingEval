
Here are five PyTorch APIs that have similar functionality to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.compat.v1.keras.initializers.RandomUniform`:

1. `torch.nn.init.uniform_`
2. `torch.nn.init.kaiming_uniform_`
3. `torch.nn.init.xavier_uniform_`
4. `torch.nn.init.normal_`
5. `torch.nn.init.truncated_normal_`
