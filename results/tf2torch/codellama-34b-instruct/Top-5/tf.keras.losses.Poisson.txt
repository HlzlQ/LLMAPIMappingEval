
Here are five possible PyTorch APIs that correspond to the TensorFlow API `tensorflow.keras.losses.Poisson`:

1. `torch.nn.functional.poisson_nll_loss`
2. `torch.nn.functional.poisson_log_likelihood`
3. `torch.nn.functional.poisson_cross_entropy`
4. `torch.nn.functional.poisson_hinge_loss`
5. `torch.nn.functional.poisson_softmax_cross_entropy`
