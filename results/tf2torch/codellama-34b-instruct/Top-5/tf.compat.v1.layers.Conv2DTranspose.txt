
Here are five possible PyTorch APIs that can be used for the same functionality as TensorFlow's `tensorflow.compat.v1.layers.Conv2DTranspose`:

1. `torch.nn.ConvTranspose2d`
2. `torch.nn.functional.conv_transpose2d`
3. `torch.nn.modules.conv.ConvTranspose2d`
4. `torch.nn.modules.conv.ConvTranspose2dWithBias`
5. `torch.nn.modules.conv.ConvTranspose2dWithoutBias`
